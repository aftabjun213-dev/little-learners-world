# 🎨 Little Learners World — Channel Setup (copy-paste guide)

Our uploader can only upload videos, so these channel-branding items must be
pasted in by hand once. Do it in **YouTube Studio → Customization**. Takes ~10 min.

---

## 1. Channel Description (About)
YouTube Studio → **Customization → Basic info → Description**. Paste this:

```
Welcome to Little Learners World — a bright, cozy place where kids ages 3 to 7 learn, play, grow, and shine! 🌈

Every single day we release a brand-new animated cartoon that makes learning feel like playtime. Your little one will explore colors, numbers, shapes, animals, feelings, good manners, and the wonderful world around them — through gentle stories, lovable characters, and catchy moments they'll want to watch again and again.

Our episodes are made with love for toddlers, preschoolers, and kindergarteners: warm storytelling, soothing music, and playful learning that helps build early vocabulary, counting, and curiosity. It's screen time parents can feel good about — always kind, always safe.

🎈 A NEW episode every day
🎨 Colors  🔢 Numbers  🔺 Shapes  🐮 Animals  ❤️ Feelings  🌟 Good Manners
👶 Perfect for ages 3–7 (preschool, toddlers & kindergarten)

Subscribe and join the Little Learners World family — let's learn something wonderful together today! 💛
```

---

## 2. Channel Keywords
YouTube Studio → **Settings → Channel → Basic info → Keywords**. Paste this
(these help YouTube understand and recommend your channel):

```
kids learning, educational cartoons for kids, toddler videos, preschool learning, learn colors for kids, learn numbers for kids, shapes for kids, animal sounds for kids, counting for kids, nursery, kindergarten learning, learning videos for toddlers, kids cartoon, baby learning, little learners world, songs for littles, educational videos for kids, learn for kids
```

---

## 3. Set the channel as "Made for Kids" (important!)
YouTube Studio → **Settings → Channel → Advanced settings → Audience** →
select **"Yes, set this channel as made for kids."** (Keeps you compliant.)

---

## 4. Country
Same Advanced settings page → **Country of residence → United States**
(aligns the channel with your main target audience). 

---

## 5. Channel Trailer + Featured Video
Customization → **Layout**:
- **Video spotlight → Channel trailer** (shown to NEW visitors): pick your best
  performer — right now that's **"What Sound Does a Cow Make?"** (your top video).
- **Featured video** (shown to returning subscribers): pick your newest/best.

---

## 6. Banner tagline (channel art)
If you make a banner later, use this tagline text:
> **Little Learners World — Learn • Play • Grow • Shine 🌈  |  New cartoons every day!**

---

## 7. Playlists (do this as videos build up — big for watch time)
Create these playlists (Content → Playlists → New) and add matching videos.
Kids auto-play through playlists, which boosts total watch time a lot:
- 🎨 Learn Colors
- 🔢 Numbers & Counting
- 🔺 Shapes
- 🐮 Animals & Sounds
- ❤️ Feelings & Kindness
- 🌈 Story Time
